About Us

The BAC is dedicated to raising awareness, provide support, and further adoption of digital currencies such as bitcoin, and blockchain technologies among Canadian consumers, merchants, businesses, regulators, and policy makers; and to promote studies and research and support participation in Canadian and international partnerships and associations.

Events and Community

Advancements in digital technology are often created in person.

Startup Mentorship

We connect startups utilizing cutting edge technology with mentors to take their business to the next level.

Education

We help educate people on the principles, possibilities and systems of blockchain technology.

R & D

We are comprised of technophiles working on new POCs and writing research papers in a variety of industries.

Regulation

We represent the industry at a national level and are committed to ensuring that blockchain technology can thrive.

Communication

We are the voice of blockchain in Canada and can be counted on to provide expertise on demand.
